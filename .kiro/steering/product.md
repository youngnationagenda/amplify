# Product Overview

Net Tribe Carbon is a platform that transforms electric motorcycle rides into verified carbon credits. The system connects IoT-equipped electric motorcycles to a carbon credit marketplace.

## Core Concepts

- **Riders** complete electric motorcycle trips that are validated via IoT telemetry
- **Carbon Credits** are generated from verified rides based on distance and energy savings
- **Investors** purchase carbon credits through an Initial Carbon Offering (ICO) model or direct marketplace
- **Offsetters** buy and burn credits to offset their carbon footprint
- **DeFi / Web3** integration enables asset-backed tokens and on-chain transactions (Celo network)

## User Roles

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Rider | Electric motorcycle operator earning carbon credits |
| Investor | Purchases credits via ICO or marketplace |
| Offsetter | Buys and burns credits for carbon offset certificates |
| Admin | Full platform management access |

## Key Flows

1. Ride → IoT validation → Carbon credit minting
2. ICO creation → Investor purchase → Credit delivery
3. Credit purchase → Burn → Certificate generation
